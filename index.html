About Us

Matthew Mohamed, Jake Paccione, and Leonard Weber are undergraduate Computer Science students at Stevens Institute of Technology. Often collaborating on in-school assignments, they decided to unite their passions into a personal project that revolved around fitness. Together, they created JACKED -- a fitness website provides the user with a FREE well rounded workout plan to users as well as a system that allows users to track their progress in the gym.
